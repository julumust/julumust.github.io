Vinicius Dias

I am researcher at LTS Research, previously at the University of Nottingham. I was duly admitted to the degree of Master of Engineering in Aerospace Engineering.

My interests lie in the intersection of neuroscience and statistical learning. How are they correlated? What simplifications can we make? Can we discern the stochastic nature of psychiatric illness by leveraging machine learning techniques?

Novel solutions require the characterisation of principles that govern our brain. A prospective framework to answer this questions lies in the application of Bayesian inference utilising transformer models as our primary network.
